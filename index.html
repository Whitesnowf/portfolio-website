Real Estate Economics Dissertation

View Project View Presentation

My undergraduate dissertation tests whether the opening of the Elizabeth Line in 2022 has caused nearby housing prices to increase. Although average price uplifts of 3.15%, 5.31% and 3.01% are estimated during the line's announcement, construction and post-opening phases respectively, there is significant variation depending on a transaction's relative price and location. Hedonic pricing models, difference-in-differences, spatial difference-in-differences, difference-in-difference-in-differences and even machine learning models are utilised to dissect and discuss results.

My supervisor and I are currently looking at publishing this paper.

S&P 500 Prediction

View Project Download Model

As part of the CamSIF application process, we were required to estimate the value of the S&P 500 index at close of 31 December 2024 using any method we wanted. The report was limited to two pages, which was definitely its most challenging aspect. I decided to use a linear regression approach, averaging the outputs of a simple- and multi-linear regression to find a 9.86% upside.
