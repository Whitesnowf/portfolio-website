Real Estate Finance Dissertation

View Project

I am currently planning to do my third-year dissertation on the location premiums of houses near stations across the London Underground Line. Since more central areas should command higher prices, this study aims to quantify that amount and compare them both within and between popular TFL Lines. With the opening of the Elizabeth Line in 2022, it would be interesting to further estimate the value gained by houses located near the stations on this line.

S&P 500 Prediction

View Project View Model

As part of the CamSIF application process, we were required to estimate the value of the S&P 500 index at close of 31 December 2024 using any method we wanted. The report was limited to two pages, which was definitely its most challenging aspect. I decided to use a linear regression approach and averaged the outputs of a simple- and multi-linear equation. Looking back, I could have also used a cashflow-based approach.
