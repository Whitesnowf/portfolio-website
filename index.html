S&P 500 Prediction

View Project View Model

As part of the CamSIF application process, we were required to estimate the value of the S&P 500 index at close of 31 December 2024. The project was limited to two pages, which was definitely the most challenging aspect. I decided to use a linear regression approach and averaged the outputs of a simple- and multi-linear equation. Looking back, I could have also used a cashflow-based approach.
